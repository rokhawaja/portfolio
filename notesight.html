Professor/Teacher Portal

Empowering educators with AI-driven tools for lecture management and student engagement.

Features Under Development

Lecture Recording & Upload AI-Powered Summaries Flashcards & Exam Prep Q&A Portal LMS Integration
  • Lecture Recording & Upload: A seamless way for professors to record or upload lecture videos/audio directly to the platform.
  • AI-Powered Summaries: Automatic generation of structured summaries to enhance comprehension and note-taking.
  • Flashcards & Exam Prep: AI-driven creation of flashcards and exam-style questions based on lecture content.
  • Q&A Portal: An interactive Q&A system where students can ask questions and receive AI-supported responses based on past lectures.
  • Integration with LMS: Future plans include compatibility with Learning Management Systems like Blackboard and Canvas.

Research & Planning

We are conducting in-depth research and user testing to refine our approach. By collaborating with educators, we aim to:

  • Identify key challenges in current lecture recording and content management tools.
  • Ensure AI-generated study materials align with real-world teaching needs.
  • Develop an intuitive, easy-to-use interface that enhances both teaching and learning experiences.

Through pilot testing and continuous iteration, our goal is to create a professor portal that not only simplifies lecture management but also transforms passive lectures into interactive learning experiences.

This feature is currently in active development. Updates will be posted as significant milestones are reached.
